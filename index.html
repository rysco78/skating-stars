About Skating Stars

Skating Stars was started in 2009 by Dmitry Palamarchuk and Viktoria Milevska with a vision of bringing world-class Ukrainian training techniques to developing skaters in the United States. Their regimen focuses on core fundamentals to build a successful skater at the highest levels of competition, all while keeping the process fun, safe, and engaging for skaters of all ages. Recently, two-time Olympic champion Ekaterina Gordeeva joined the coaching staff. She is renowned for her unique technique, exceptional elegance, and innovative approach to figure skating.

Comprehensive Training System

Skating Stars focuses on every component of a developing figure skater, with training that supports a holistic skating education, including:

Stretch and flexibility

Olympic-level Coaches

Dmitry Palamarchuk and Victoria Milevska have a combined 35 years of coaching experience, hold masters degrees in physical and sport education, and have coached skaters on a national and international scale. Dmitry is a 2002 Olympic Competitor and 2-time Jr. World Champion, and passes along his extensive experience from training under the highest level coaches to his own students.

Learning to Skate

Skating Stars coaches are certified in the Learn to Skate USA program. They can create a plan for you or your child to advance at whatever speed you are comfortable, and will ensure the skater is in a nurturing, safe environment to discover their love for the ice. While Skating Stars primarily focuses on skaters who are currently – or aspire to become – high-level competitive figure skaters, our coaches foster skaters with a variety of goals.
